Sidhu Moosewala

About

Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, also known as Sidhu Moose Wala, was a Punjabi musician, rapper, actor, and politician.He began his career as a lyricist for Ninja’s song “License,” and then as a singer with the duet “G Wagon.” With his song “So High,” Moose Wala drew a lot of attention. His debut album, PBX 1, debuted at number 66 on the Billboard Canadian Albums chart in 2018/. He had previously been sued for promoting gun culture and utilizing aggressive and inciting lyrics in his music. Unfortunately, Moose Wala was shot dead in his car by unidentified assailants in Mansa, Punjab, on 29 May 2022. He was traveling with two friends at the time. During the incident, 30 rounds were fired.
